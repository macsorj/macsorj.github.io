Fight4Life’s 5th Year

December 26, 2017 macsorj Uncategorized 0

 

Today marks the 5th year since Fight4Life launched to the public! It has been a long, incredibly fun journey, and there will be many more great times to come in the future! The server and the community has had a huge impact on my life. It’s impossible to see how much different everything would be for me if it hadn’t existed. I’ve met some incredible people over the years through the server, and there are so many memorable events and adventures that have happened.

I hope everyone continues to help the server grow, improve, and most importantly, for everyone to have fun!
